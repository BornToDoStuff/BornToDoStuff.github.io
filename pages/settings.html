Settings

These settings use cookies in your browser to change aspects about the site.


Table Mode

Switch items between grid and table view. Table view hides images and text but fits more items on a single screen.

Detailed View

View item costs, tags, and other details that are otherwise hidden for easy viewing when visiting item pages.

Economy Scale

When Detailed View is enabled, items have a suggested cost. You can toggle between the Gold Standard Economy (a slight variant of the PHB standard) and the Historical Silver Economy (a silver-based economy with less severe price-ramp).

Advanced Search

Enable a more advanced search that is more finicky but allows for more complex filtering and sorting.
